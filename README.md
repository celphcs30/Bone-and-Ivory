# Bone and Ivory

A RimWorld mod that adds macabre decorative items, ivory crafting, and bone-based structures.

## Original Mod

This mod is an updated continuation of the abandoned mod **"SWR's Skulls and Ivory"** by **Homeless Emperor** (She Wants Revenge).

**Original Steam Workshop**: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3169742181

The original mod was abandoned after RimWorld 1.4 and has been updated for RimWorld 1.6 compatibility with improvements and bug fixes.

## Description

Bone and Ivory introduces the coveted Ivory material, prized for its quality akin to jade. Butchering wild boars yields 2 ivory while processing elephant tusks or thrumbo horns at a stonecutter provides significant amounts.

Enhancing vanilla features: Retexture and rotatable functionality for the Skullspike!

## Features

### Resources
- **Ivory**: High-value material crafted from elephant tusks and thrumbo horns
- Can be used as a stuff material for crafting

### Buildings
- **Bone Wall**: Impassable wall made of bones and skulls (500 HP, paintable, drag placement enabled)
- **Long Skullspike Torch**: Perpetual torch made from 4 skulls
- **Double Skullspike**: Decorative spike with two skulls

### Floors
- **Skull Floor**: Basic bone floor
- **Skull Fine Floor**: Ornate bone floor pattern
- **Skull Pathway**: Simple pathway made from skulls
- **Ivory Tile**: Luxury ivory flooring

### Ideology Integration
- All items contribute to **Morbid** style dominance
- Bone walls provide Morbid meditation focus
- Compatible with Ideology DLC room theme system

### Quality of Life
- All walls and floors are **paintable**
- Bone walls support drag placement (lines/squares)
- Enhanced for Combat Extended compatibility (500 HP walls)

## Compatibility

- **RimWorld Version**: 1.6, 1.5
- **DLC Required**: None (Ideology DLC recommended for full features)
- **Mod Conflicts**: None known

## Installation

1. Download the mod
2. Place the `CelphIvoryAndSkulls` folder in your RimWorld `Mods` directory
3. Enable the mod in RimWorld's mod menu
4. Load your game
